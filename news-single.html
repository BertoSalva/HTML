Net Rippers BC Takes Flight at the Eagles League

By Net Rippers Media
May 2025

We’re excited to introduce Net Rippers BC — a fresh and passionate youth basketball club based in Norwood, Johannesburg. With dedicated U16 and U18 teams, we’ve officially joined the Saint John's Eagles League to test our grit and talent.

Training out of Paterson Park, our young athletes are committed to developing modern, positionless basketball with strong fundamentals, teamwork, and flair. Our goal isn’t just to win — it's to help elevate South African basketball from the grassroots up.

"Every session, every game — we're not just playing, we're building something bigger."
— Coach, Net Rippers BC

We believe in hustle over hype and work ethic over excuses. Whether it's game day or practice, our team is learning, growing, and showing up with purpose. Follow our journey as we grind through our first season and plant our roots in the SA basketball scene.

If you're in Norwood, come catch us training at Paterson Park or showing out in the Eagles League — this is just the beginning.
