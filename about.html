ABOUT THE CLUB

WELCOME TO NET RIPPERS BC

Net Rippers Basketball Club is a dynamic youth-centered sports club based in South Africa. We focus on building fundamental basketball skills, game intelligence, and personal discipline in aspiring players across age categories from U14 to U18.

Our training philosophy combines modern techniques with core values — teamwork, leadership, and dedication. Through weekly training sessions, regional competitions, and structured development programs, we aim to shape not just great athletes, but great people.

CLUB HISTORY

Net Rippers Basketball Club has grown from a community initiative into a youth development powerhouse, shaping South Africa’s next generation of basketball talent through discipline, teamwork, and modern training.

FOUNDATION OF THE DREAM

A group of hoopers in their prime wanted to find a way to play basketball in the lockdown period and joined a Fiba 3v3 league where they played top teams, some international and the group was known for shooting the lights out (or ripping the nets)

IDEA PAUSED

The movement was holted as the initial members who started the dream moved into different spaces in life

OFFICIAL LAUNCH

The official movement to the clubs existence where it made it's debut to the Eagles league and is growing in strength and recognition week in and week out

FUTURE PROJECTION: ACADEMY GROWTH

By 2026, Net Rippers aims to have more age groups, providing full-time development, attend more tournaments, and talent exposure pipelines for players aspiring to go pro or use it as a platform for tertiarty opportunities.

ABOUT NET RIPPERS BC

We’re a youth basketball club based in Norwood, training at Paterson Park. Competing in the Saint John's Eagles League with U16 and U18 teams, we’re committed to developing modern basketball talent and growing the sport in South Africa.
