FAQ

When and where are the practice sessions held?

Practice sessions are held every Wednesday from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM at Paterson Park Multipurpose Centre. Be on time, come ready to train, and bring your A-game!

What age groups does Net Rippers cater to?

We train and develop players in the following age divisions: Under 14, Under 15, Under 16, and Under 18. Each group follows a training plan suited to their level and potential.

What does training at Net Rippers focus on?

Our training is rooted in basketball fundamentals — dribbling, passing, shooting, defense, and teamwork. We believe in building strong foundations to shape elite athletes.

Where are your games usually played?

Our league matches are usually hosted at venues like St John’s College, St Benedict’s, and St Stithians. Friendly games are organized at various locations depending on team availability.

How can I join Net Rippers or get more information?

You can visit our Contact page to reach out, or connect with us via our social media pages listed in the footer. We’re happy to welcome new talent and supporters!

ABOUT CLUB

Net Rippers BC is a youth basketball development club committed to building positionless, modern basketball players. We focus on discipline, skill, team identity, and creating opportunities for young athletes to elevate their game across South Africa.
