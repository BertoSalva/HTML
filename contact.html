ABOUT CLUB

Net Rippers BC is a youth basketball club competing in the Saint John's Eagles League. Based in Norwood and training at Paterson Park, we run U16 and U18 squads dedicated to developing talent, discipline, and the future of South African basketball through a modern, positionless team style.
